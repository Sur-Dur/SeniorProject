How to Use SürDur

A step-by-step guide to unlock the full potential of our sustainability platform

Welcome to the SürDur user guide! Our platform makes sustainable living easy and accessible for everyone. Follow these simple steps to get started and make a positive impact on our planet.

Getting Started

01

Download and Install

Start your sustainability journey by downloading the SürDur app from your device's app store. The installation process is quick and straightforward. Just tap the install button and follow the on-screen prompts.

Pro Tip:

Make sure you're connected to Wi-Fi for a faster download experience. SürDur requires iOS 12.0+ or Android 8.0+ to function optimally.

02

Create Your Account

After installation, open the app and create your personal account. You can register using your email, phone number, or social media accounts. Fill in basic information to help personalize your sustainability journey.

Pro Tip:

Choose a strong, unique password and enable two-factor authentication for added security. Your sustainability data is important!

03

Set Up Your Profile

Complete your profile by adding your sustainability interests, goals, and current lifestyle habits. This information helps us customize recommendations specifically for you. You can always update these later.

Pro Tip:

Be honest about your current habits to get the most accurate baseline for improvement. The more detailed information you provide, the more personalized your experience will be.

Using Core Features

04

Track Your Carbon Footprint

Navigate to the "Footprint" tab in the bottom menu. Here you can log daily activities like transportation, energy use, and consumption habits. The app automatically calculates your carbon footprint and shows your impact over time.

Pro Tip:

Enable location services for automatic transportation tracking, and connect smart home devices for real-time energy monitoring. Try to log activities daily for the most accurate results.

05

Join Sustainability Challenges

Explore the "Challenges" section to find daily, weekly, and monthly sustainability challenges. Join those that interest you and track your progress. Complete challenges to earn points and badges while making real environmental impact.

Pro Tip:

Start with easier challenges and gradually take on more difficult ones. Invite friends to join the same challenges - group participation increases completion rates by 78%!

06

Connect with the Community

Tap on the "Community" tab to join sustainability forums, share your experiences, and learn from others. Participate in discussions, post your achievements, and get inspired by what others in your community are doing.

Pro Tip:

Join local groups based on your city or region to find nearby sustainability events and initiatives. Community involvement can double your individual impact.

Advanced Features

07

Get Personalized Recommendations

Visit the "For You" section to discover AI-powered sustainability recommendations tailored to your lifestyle. These suggestions are based on your habits, location, and sustainability goals.

Pro Tip:

Rate the recommendations you receive to help the AI learn your preferences better. The more you interact with this feature, the more accurate it becomes.

08

Generate Impact Reports

Access detailed reports about your sustainability journey from the "Impact" tab. View weekly, monthly, and yearly stats showing your progress, carbon savings, and contribution to various UN Sustainable Development Goals.

Pro Tip:

Share your impact reports on social media to inspire others. You can also export them as PDFs for presentations or personal records.

Congratulations! You're now ready to make full use of the SürDur platform. Remember, every small action counts towards a more sustainable future. If you need additional help, visit our FAQ section or contact our support team through the app.
