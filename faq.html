Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about SürDur

What is SürDur?

SürDur is a Türkiye-based navigation and trip planner app that offers personalized stopover recommendations along your driving route.

How is SürDur different from other navigation apps?

Unlike standard navigation apps, SürDur suggests unique, personalized stopovers such as scenic spots, local eateries, or cultural landmarks to enhance your trip experience.

Is SürDur only available in Türkiye?

Yes, currently SürDur is designed exclusively for routes within Türkiye to ensure local accuracy and quality recommendations.

Can I use SürDur offline?

No. SürDur requires a constant internet connection for routing, maps, and place data.

How do the personalized stopovers work?

SürDur analyzes your route and preferences (e.g., nature, history, food) to suggest meaningful stopovers along the way.

Does SürDur support turn-by-turn navigation?

Yes, SürDur has built-in turn-by-turn navigation so you can travel without needing to switch between apps.

Can I share my routes with friends or family?

Yes, SürDur includes a social page where you can share your saved routes, view others' journeys, and get inspired.

Which languages does SürDur support?

SürDur is currently available in English. Full Turkish language support will be added at the official launch.
